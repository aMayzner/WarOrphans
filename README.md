# War Orphans

A RimWorld mod that adds events where war-torn settlements beg you to take in their orphaned children.

Requires RimWorld 1.5/1.6 and Biotech DLC.

## Quests

### War Orphans
1-7 children from a non-hostile faction's settlement, fleeing war. Mixed xenotypes matching the faction's distribution. Sibling families with shared dead parents.

### Persecuted Orphans
All children share the same non-Baseliner xenotype — they're being hunted for what they are. Rarer variant with different narrative.

### Sole Survivor
A single child, the only one left alive. Max trauma, severe malnutrition, multiple injuries, 40% chance of a missing body part. Bonus survival skills. 20% chance of a retribution raid days later.

## What the Children Come With

- **War trauma** — scales with age, fades over ~100 days. Affects mood, mental breaks, learning, sleep, social behavior
- **Recovery milestones** — positive thoughts and letters as trauma heals: "the nightmares are less frequent", "starting to feel safe", "this place is home"
- **Dead parents** visible in social tab
- **Malnutrition**, cuts, tattered/tainted clothes
- **Small chance of missing body part** (older children)
- **"Escaped war together"** — permanent +25 opinion between orphans
- **Mutual gratitude** — orphans and colonists get positive social thoughts toward each other

## Storyteller

- Charity events with accept/reject letter (not quest tab)
- Population-aware, not threat-gated
- +15 faction goodwill on accept
- Ideology charity integration
